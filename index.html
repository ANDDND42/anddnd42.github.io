Metehan Akkuyu — Team Lead Front-End Developer

Over more than four years at MeetMatch (2020–2025) — a B2B event-technology platform that used AI-driven matchmaking to connect professionals at conferences and trade shows — I worked as a front-end developer and, in my final two years, as Team Lead for front-end development. I owned the front-end architecture of the core product, led and mentored a team of three developers (including recruitment and qualification), and worked directly with clients in an Agile setup to design and ship new features. The stack was JavaScript, AngularJS and Node.js (Express), with Pug for templating. After MeetMatch was acquired, the product lives on today as Apella.

The work I'm most proud of is an automated visual-regression testing system I designed and built. Using Puppeteer to drive the application across multiple devices and pages, paired with image comparison to flag any unintended change to the UI, it replaced a manual test pass that used to take around an hour. A full check now runs in under three minutes — with no risk of a broken layout slipping through or a (sub)page being forgotten.

Python — TraderBot

TraderBot is a Python package that implements and backtests a custom VIDYA + OTT crossover trading strategy on cryptocurrency data. It integrates with Bybit via CCXT to fetch OHLCV candlestick data, runs configurable backtests with leverage, take-profit, and stop-loss settings, and outputs detailed performance statistics.

Keywords: Technical Indicators, Backtesting Engine, Visual Mode, Data Handling, Modern Python Project
